Ruijie Gao

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor Ann Arbor, MI, USA

I am a PhD student supervised by Prof. Nathanial Bleier, in the Computer Science and Engineering Division (CSE) at the University of Michigan. My research focuses on computer architecture and electronic design automation , with particular emphasis on CPU μArch, formal verification &logic synthesis, and agile hardware design.

Previously, I completed my B.Eng. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the joint program by University of Glasgow and UESTC. I worked closely with Prof. Jian Weng from KAUST, Dr. Yu Zeng during my undergraduate studies.
